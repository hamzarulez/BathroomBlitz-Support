Privacy Policy for Bathroom Blitz

Last Updated: September 5, 2025

This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information for the Bathroom Blitz mobile application (the "Game").

1. Data Collection

We believe in your privacy. Bathroom Blitz is designed to be a simple, fun game that respects your personal information. As such, we do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from our players. The game does not include any analytics, trackers, or third-party SDKs that would gather data about you or your device.

2. Game Data

Any data related to your game progress, such as high scores, will be shown.

3. Children's Privacy

Our Game does not knowingly collect personal information. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, we are in full compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
